Choosing the greatest footballer of all time is a challenging task, as it involves comparing players from different eras and with different styles. However, let's analyze the achievements and impacts of Pelé, Messi, and Maradona.

Pelé is a three-time World Cup winner with Brazil and is known for his speed, skill, and scoring ability. He won the World Cup at a young age and is considered one of the greatest players of his generation.

Maradona is also a World Cup winner and is known for his exceptional skill, vision, and leadership. He is remembered for his 'Hand of God' and 'Goal of the Century' in the 1986 World Cup. Maradona's impact on the game was significant, and he is still revered by many as one of the greatest players of all time.

Messi, on the other hand, has consistently performed at an elite level for over a decade and has won numerous individual and team honors, including six Ballon d'Or awards and four UEFA Champions League titles. He is known for his incredible dribbling skills, vision, and goal-scoring ability.

While all three players are incredibly talented and have achieved great success, Messi's consistency, longevity, and dominance at the highest level set him apart. He has won numerous titles and individual awards, and his impact on the game has been significant.

FINAL ANSWER: Messi
