This is one of the greatest debates in football history, and reasonable people can disagree. Let me break it down:

**Case for Messi:**
- More Ballon d'Or awards (8 vs 5)
- Superior playmaking ability — elite-level assists and creativity
- Won the 2022 World Cup (and was tournament's best player)
- Won the Copa América (2021)
- More consistent dribbling and close control, widely regarded as the most naturally gifted player ever
- Higher peak performance in terms of combined goals + assists
- Dominated La Liga and Champions League statistical records alongside Ronaldo

**Case for Ronaldo:**
- Incredible goalscoring record across multiple top leagues (England, Spain, Italy)
- More Champions League goals (all-time top scorer)
- Won the Euros with Portugal (2016)
- Arguably the greatest physical specimen in football history — speed, power, heading, both feet
- Remarkable longevity and dedication to fitness
- Proved himself in more leagues than Messi

**Key Differentiator:**
Messi's all-around game — the combination of dribbling, vision, passing, and scoring — gives him an edge in terms of pure footballing completeness. His World Cup victory in 2022 largely settled the debate for many analysts and fans. Statistically, Messi leads in most combined offensive metrics.

FINAL ANSWER: Messi
