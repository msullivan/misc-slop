Messi and Ronaldo are both extraordinary footballers, each possessing a unique blend of talent, skill, and achievements that have reshaped the sport.

**Messi**  
- **Playmaking & Vision**: Often celebrated for his uncanny ability to read the game, deliver pinpoint passes, and create chances for teammates.  
- **Dribbling & Agility**: His low centre of gravity, close ball control, and rapid changes of direction make him arguably the most elusive dribbler in football history.  
- **Records & Awards**: Seven‑time Ballon d’Or winner, all‑time top scorer for a single club (FC Barcelona), and numerous La Liga and Champions League titles.  
- **Consistency**: Over a 15‑year span at the highest level, he has maintained astonishing goal‑per‑game ratios and a high number of assists.

**Ronaldo**  
- **Physical Power & Heading**: Combines speed, strength, and aerial ability, making him a prolific scorer from open play, set‑pieces, and headers.  
- **Goal‑Scoring Efficiency**: Over 800 career goals (as of early 2026), multiple season‑by‑season Golden Boot awards, and consistent high scoring rates across leagues (Premier League, La Liga, Serie A).  
- **Versatility**: Effective as a winger, striker, or second forward; adapts his game to different tactical systems.  
- **Professionalism & Longevity**: Remarkable durability, maintaining elite performance into his late‑30s, with titles in England, Spain, Italy, and Portugal.

Both have shattered records, inspired generations, and brought joy to fans worldwide. Their differing styles mean that “better” often hinges on personal preference—whether one values Messi’s creative genius and consistency or Ronaldo’s athletic prowess and goal‑scoring versatility.

**FINAL ANSWER: Messi**
